The goal of this project was to design, print, and finish a custom bookmark for Pequea Elementary School Library.

To start the process of creating this bookmark, I first drew thumbnail sketches to generate Ideas for potential bookmark designs.

Next, I picked a design from my thumbnails to use and created a rough layout in Adobe inDesign.

Then, I made changes to my bookmark design and created a comprehensive layout of my final artwork.

Finally, I printed my bookmarks on the offset lithography printing press and cut out the bookmarks so that they were all the desired size.

The purpose for this project was to research a communication technology and create an infographic representing how this technology fits the communication technology model and creates an understanding of how the technology operates.

For my communication technology, I chose to research the electric guitar. On the body if the electric guitar, there are pickups located underneath the strings that generate a magnetic field, magnetizing the strings of the guitar. When a person plays the electric guitar, the strings vibrate, which is detected by a coil of wire in the pickups and causes a small electric current to be generated. This current is sent through a cord that connects the electric guitar to an amplifier. The amplifier then boosts the electric current and the loudspeaker turns this current into sound.

To create my infographic, I used Adobe Illustrator. To gather my information, I used the following sources:

yamaha.com
howstuffworks.com
explainthatstuff.com
guitar-skill-builder.com reverb.com
invention.si.edu
